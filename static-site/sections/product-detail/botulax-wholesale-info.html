Storage, Safety & Professional Handling

Botulax products are handled under refrigerated conditions at 2°C to 8°C with cold-chain shipping controls. Professional-use restrictions vary by country, and all clinics and distributors should review official manufacturer documentation and destination-market requirements before procurement and handling.
