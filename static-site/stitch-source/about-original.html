Who We Are

With a prestigious 15-year history rooted in the medical heart of South Korea, Medical Booster has evolved from a local distributor into a global powerhouse of aesthetic excellence.

Our journey is built on unwavering trust and deep-seated relationships with nearly all major Korean manufacturers, giving us unparalleled access to the newest breakthroughs before they reach the general market.

Uniquely, we operate our own state-of-the-art production laboratory. This allows us to not only distribute but also rigorously test and refine the solutions we provide, ensuring every product meets the highest clinical benchmarks.

From the Heart of Seoul to the World's Best Clinics

Established in the bustling medical district of Gangnam, Seoul, Medical Booster began with a simple observation: the world's most innovative aesthetic technologies were often the hardest to source reliably.

We set out to bridge this gap by building a robust platform that combines the agility of a digital marketplace with the rigorous quality standards of a traditional medical distributor.

Today, we serve a global network of dermatologists and medical spas, helping them stay at the forefront of their field through timely access to the latest Korean advancements in fillers, toxins, and medical devices.
