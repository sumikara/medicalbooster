description Overview

Botulax is a peripheral muscle relaxant that contains purified botulinum toxin type A (in combination with haemagglutinin) and sodium chloride (excipient). It is indicated for the treatment of benign essential blepharospasm in patients 18 years of age and older.

Application & Usage

science

1. Preparation

Ensure the treatment area is properly cleansed and disinfected. Assess patient anatomy and target muscle groups before proceeding.

water_drop

2. Reconstitution

Reconstitute the 100 Unit vial with 2.5ml of sterile, preservative-free 0.9% Sodium Chloride injection to achieve 4U/0.1ml concentration.

vaccines

3. Administration

Administer using a 30G needle. Inject precise units into targeted facial muscles (e.g., corrugator supercilii) following standard aesthetic protocols.
